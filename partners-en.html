The General Partner of PLISMUN20

Why are we looking for partners?

We are looking for partners who could help us organise an international Model United Nations conference for high school students. This conference will be taking place early 2022 in the historical heart of Europe – Prague. We are expecting 150 delegates to attend the conference from around the world as well as from Prague. This is an excellent opportunity for your company to gain attention from a young and dynamic demographic

Finance

We are looking for partners who would be able to help us with the financial side of the conference. We are welcome to any sponsorships. PLISMUN is a nonprofit organisation; any money received will be used solely for organisational purposes.

Services

Food and accommodation are an integral part of the conference. We are looking for companies that can provide us with services for our delegates, such as catering, accommodation, ceremony venues and more.

Materials

We are looking for suppliers of printing services and stationery for our delegates, such as customised pens, badges, and more.

Why choose PLISMUN22?

  •       Four previous editions of the PLISMUN conference have proved our consistent ability to organize successful events

  •       PLISMUN'22 is completely student-led and organized by a passionate and driven team

  •       PLISMUN'19, '20 and '21 were extremely successful events that resulted in high demand from international and domestic schools alike. We are expecting even more this year!

  •       We represent a private international school, Park Lane International School, and we are driven to surpass last year's performance.

  •       Our goal is to encourage young people to become global citizens that are knowledgeable across a wide range of subjects.

  •       PLISMUN 2022 is a stellar way to support the community and to improve brand awareness on a global scale

What We Offer
